You're looking at the Dell Alienware 17 Gaming laptop. You shouldn't ignore this laptop because it's among the most powerful laptops around. Everything about it is top-notch, starting with its processor. It runs an Intel Core i9 processor with 32GB of RAM, which is unheard of, even on a gaming PC but it's present in this compact setup. There is an overclocked version of the NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1080 on this laptop which makes it faster than other regular GeForce GTX 1080 graphics cards. The storage is a total of 2TB, 1TB on a hyper-fast solid-state drive, while the other 1TB is on a hard drive. The 1TB SSD should launch heavy gaming titles in seconds, while the hard disk is best used for storing bulk data, such as movies, music and photos. The 17.3-inch display is among the largest in the market, and it runs a QHD resolution (2560x1440) which is even better than full HD (1920 x 1080). This means you still get a crisp look and detail of a high-density display. Everything about this laptop oozes performance, and whether that’s a good thing. Whether you're a hardcore gamer or a professional 3D and graphics artist, this is the laptop you need.
